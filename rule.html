Level System

Each user has a level, ranging from lv.1 to lv.6, Gaining enough exp can let you get to the next level, see:

Level Exp Needed
lv.1 0
lv.2 10
lv.3 50
lv.4 100
lv.5 200
lv.6 500

How to gain exp?

  • Every time you have done a daily check-in, your exp will increase by 5.
  • You can receive 10 exp for every question you answered.
  • Once an ansnwer is accpeted, you will get another 20 exp.

Why pursue higher level?

Some questions may block to lower level users and a highest level can guarantee a full view of the forum. On the other hand, you can set level limit up to your level when you asking a question!

Gold System

Asker can offer a reward for the question. The bounty gold will be sent to whom whose answer get accpeted. To attract others answer your question, don't hesitate to paying some gold for it! On top of that, the amount of gold is open to everyone, it can show a user's prestige.
