Level System

Each user has a level, ranging from lv.1 to lv.6, Gaining enough exp can let you get to the next level, see:

Level Exp Needed
lv.1 0
lv.2 10
lv.3 50
lv.4 100
lv.5 200
lv.6 500

How to gain exp?

  • Every time you have done a daily check-in, your exp will increase by 5.
  • You can receive 10 exp for every question you answered.
  • Once an ansnwer is accpeted, you will get another 20 exp.

Why pursue higher level?

Some questions may be blocked to lower level users; a highest level can guarantee a full view of the forum. On the other hand, you can set a level limit when you are asking a question to prevent spam answers!

For example:
If you are level 2, you will not be able to view the answers to a question with level 3+ restriction.
If you want to view the answers to a question with Level 5 restriction, you need to spend 3 golds (5 - 2) to unlock this quesiton.

Gold System

How to gain gold?

  • You will receive 1 gold every time you have done a daily check-in.
  • You can get reward gold from others by giving accepted answers of good quality.

Why do you need gold?

You can offer a reward for the question. The bounty gold will be sent to whom whose answer get accpeted. This can make your question more attractive. On top of that, the amount of gold is open to everyone, it can show your prestige.
